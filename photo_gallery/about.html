About

Photo Gallery is a calm place to view themed photography. The site emphasizes readability, consistent alignment, and predictable navigation so images remain the focus on both mobile and desktop.

Concept

This site is designed around a simple idea: small, curated sets feel more intentional than endless feeds. Themes keep the experience cohesive, and short captions provide context without turning the site into a blog.

Theme guidelines

Collections are organized by clear visual directions such as light, color, composition, texture, and atmosphere. A visitor should be able to understand the overall direction of a set quickly and then explore details at their own pace.

Design principles

The layout prioritizes calm rhythm, comfortable spacing, and strong contrast for readability. Images are optimized for web to support fast loading. The interface stays minimal so attention remains on photography rather than controls.

Next steps

The project remains a static site without databases or external data. Future improvements can expand collections, refine captions, and adjust layout details based on wireframes while keeping the structure simple and maintainable.
