Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this White Water Rafting website is to clearly present trip options, safety standards, pricing, and booking instructions so that first-time and returning adventurers can confidently select and reserve a guided rafting tour. The site will build trust with safety credentials and reviews, answer FAQs concisely, and drive conversions through a streamlined “Book Now” flow.

Audience

Primary audience: ages 20–40 who enjoy outdoor recreation and want a safe, memorable weekend experience with friends or family. Many are price-conscious, compare options on mobile, and look for clear safety information, gear lists, and professional guides. Secondary audience: corporate/school groups seeking team-building packages. Most users will access via mobile and laptop; content and CTAs will be optimized for both.
