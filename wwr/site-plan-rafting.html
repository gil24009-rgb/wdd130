Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this White Water Rafting website is to clearly present trip options, safety standards, pricing, and booking instructions so that first-time and returning adventurers can confidently select and reserve a guided rafting tour.

Audience

Primary audience: ages 20–40 who enjoy outdoor recreation and want a safe, memorable weekend experience with friends or family. Secondary audience: corporate and school groups seeking team-building packages.
