Overview

Purpose

Present a research-driven design portfolio highlighting UI/UX case studies, graphic design projects, and a transparent problem-solving process. The site aims to communicate method, craft, and impact through concise narratives and evidence-based outcomes.

Audience

Recruiters and hiring managers for design internships or junior roles, BYU–Idaho faculty evaluating coursework, and peers seeking collaboration or critique. Visitors value clarity, credible process, and accessible navigation across projects.
